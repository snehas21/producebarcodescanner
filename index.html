PLU stickers on loose produce are 4-5 digit numbers (not always a scannable barcode) - type them in directly. Packaged items use a longer 8-14 digit barcode.

PLU lookups use a small hand-checked reference list, not the full official IFPS database - always confirm anything that matters against store signage. Barcode (UPC/EAN) lookups query the free, crowd-sourced Open Food Facts database and may not cover every Canadian product.

In Canada, produce sold as "organic" must be certified under the Canada Organic Regime (CFIA) - look for the Canada Organic logo.
